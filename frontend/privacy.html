Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, and password when you create an account. We also collect resume content and job description text you paste into our tools, solely to provide you with AI-generated results.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process payments through Stripe
  • Provide AI-powered resume, cover letter, job match, and interview prep features
  • Send transactional emails such as password reset
  • Improve our service

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — for payment processing. Your payment details are handled directly by Stripe and never stored on our servers.
  • OpenAI — to generate AI-powered resume and career content. Text you submit may be sent to OpenAI's API.
  • Render — for hosting our application.
  • MongoDB Atlas — for database storage.

5. Cookies

We use localStorage to store your authentication token. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

6. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us at support@rolerocketai.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

RoleRocket AI is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at support@rolerocketai.com.
