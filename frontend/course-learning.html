        Frequently asked questions

        When will I have access to the modules and assignments?

        Access begins immediately. Modules unlock sequentially as you demonstrate mastery. Complete the placement diagnostic to start your adaptive learning path.

        What is the Mastery Gate system?

        Each module requires (1) passing a 3-question quiz with 2/3 correct, and (2) completing a written Transfer Task demonstrating real-world application. Both must pass before the next module unlocks — mirroring Coursera's graded assignment model.

        Do I get academic credit for completing this course?

        No. This course is for personal learning and exam preparation only. It does not grant academic credit, CEUs/CPD hours, or institutional certification unless your school, employer, or exam body explicitly confirms otherwise.

        How do the Coding Labs work?

        Each module includes a Coding Lab panel linking to a Google Colab notebook. Click "Open Notebook in Colab" to complete the hands-on Python assignment, then return here to submit the module quiz and transfer task.
