It is designed for active job seekers, career changers, students, veterans, and institution cohorts.
RoleRocket AI serves individual job seekers at all career levels, students (including CSEC/CAPE learners), veterans, recruiters, and educational institutions looking to track and support student career outcomes. We also have dedicated tools for the Jamaican and Caribbean job market.

Features

The ATS Optimizer analyzes your resume against a target job description, scores it for keyword compatibility and formatting, then returns prioritized suggestions to maximize your chance of passing Applicant Tracking System filters before a recruiter sees your application.
The AI Career Coach provides personalized, structured coaching sessions that turn your background and goals into a clear, winning strategy. It helps you identify skill gaps, craft your professional narrative, and build a plan to reach your next role.
The Jamaica Workforce Accelerator is a dedicated hub for Jamaican and Caribbean job seekers. It includes Jamaica-specific job market radar, resume localization scoring, interview prep for regional employers, career pathway guides, scholarship finders, and a skills-gap report tool.
The Cohort Manager lets universities, training programs, and workforce agencies track their student cohorts on the platform. Admins can view activation rates, job tracking activity, ATS scores, and overall cohort readiness — all from a single dashboard. Institutions get unique invite codes to onboard students directly.
Our job search surfaces relevant listings from major job boards including LinkedIn and Indeed. We do not automate applications on your behalf — you apply deliberately to roles you choose, which protects your account reputation and ensures each application is quality-controlled.

Account & Access

On the login page, click Forgot password? and enter your account email. You will receive a reset link valid for 1 hour. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder. Contact Support if you still can't access your account.
For security, RoleRocket AI automatically logs you out after 60 minutes of inactivity. This protects your account if you step away from a shared or public device. Simply log back in to resume your session.
If your university, training program, or employer provided you with an institution invite code, use the link they gave you or visit Sign Up and enter the invite code in the "Institution Invite Code" field. This links your account to your cohort and may unlock Elite-level access for the duration of your institution's program.
Institution administrators have access to the Cohort Manager and Admin Invites pages via the sidebar. From there you can generate invite codes, monitor student activation and activity, view cohort KPIs, and export data. Contact Support if you need your admin access enabled.
