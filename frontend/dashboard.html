❓ Frequently Asked Questions


        

          
How does 1-Click Apply work?

          
We prioritize your best jobs and prepare your application flow faster so you can move on strong opportunities with less friction.

        


        

          
Do I need to rewrite my resume every time?

          
No. RoleRocket helps tailor your resume to each role using AI so you can move faster without starting from scratch.

        


        

          
What makes this different from Jobscan?

          
RoleRocket does more than scoring. It helps you find jobs, tailor applications, prep for interviews, track your pipeline, and move faster.

        


        

          
Is Lifetime access really forever?

          
Yes. Lifetime is a one-time payment that unlocks all current features and future upgrades included in the plan.

        


        

          
Can I start free?

          
Yes. You can start free, explore the workflow, and upgrade only when you want deeper automation and guidance.

        


        

          
How do referrals work?

          
Share your unique invite link. At 3 referrals you unlock Pro, at 10 referrals you unlock Premium, and at 25 referrals you unlock Lifetime.
